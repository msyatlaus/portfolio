About Bradley

Raised in the livley bayou community of Houma, Louisiana, Bradley is proud of his Cajun roots. After attending Louisana State University, Bradley travelled the world, while working as a contractor for the Department of Defense, in an attempt to connect with other curtual influences. As a result, he cultivated a better understanding of the world and attributes much of his current perspective to his adventures. Bradley has been a 'weird' Austinite for over a decade and loves to explore the local nature features of the city. Live music, good food, friends, and family keep him busy. Often seeking out quirky endeavors, Bradley realizes that, as a web developer, he has a unique opportunity to contribute to the vibrant world culture he adores. Web development provides him with an outlet to explore creativity coupled with logical reasoning, for the betterment of the community.
